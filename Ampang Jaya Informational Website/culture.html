    • Malay Culture

    ❮ ❯

    The Malay culture is known for its rich traditions, vibrant festivals, and distinctive arts and crafts. Traditional Malay music, dance, and clothing are integral parts of their cultural heritage.

    Chinese Culture

    ❮ ❯

    The Chinese community in Malaysia has preserved its cultural heritage through various festivals, cuisines, and traditional practices. Chinese New Year, the Mooncake Festival, and dragon dances are key cultural elements.

    Indian Culture

    ❮ ❯

    Indian culture in Malaysia is characterized by its diverse languages, religions, and traditions. Festivals like Deepavali and Thaipusam, along with Indian cuisine and classical dance, showcase the richness of Indian heritage.
